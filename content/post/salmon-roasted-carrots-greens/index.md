## Salmon with Roasted Carrots, Onion, and Greens

### Ingredients for Salmon with Roasted Carrots, Onion, and Greens

Ingredient | Quantity | How
---|---|---
Carrots | 3 | cut into sticks or coins
Onion | 1 | sliced into wedges
Olive oil | 1–2 tbsp |
Salt | to taste |
Pepper | to taste |
Thyme or parsley | a little | optional
Olive oil | 1 tbsp |
Lemon | 1/2 | juiced
Garlic | 1–2 cloves | minced or grated
Dijon mustard | 1 tsp | optional
Honey | 1 tsp | optional
Broccoli or zucchini | as needed | cut into chunks
Salmon fillets | 2 |
Lemon | 1/2 | sliced, plus extra juice
Dill or parsley | to taste | fresh

### Method for Salmon with Roasted Carrots, Onion, and Greens

#### Step 1
Heat oven to 210°C.

#### Step 2
Put **3 carrots**, **1 onion**, **1–2 tbsp olive oil**, **salt**, **pepper**, and optional **thyme or parsley** on a baking tray. Roast for 20 mins.

#### Step 3
Mix **1 tbsp olive oil**, juice from **1/2 lemon**, **1–2 garlic cloves**, **1 tsp Dijon mustard**, **1 tsp honey**, **salt**, and **pepper**.

#### Step 4
Add **broccoli or zucchini** to the tray and toss lightly with the vegetables.

#### Step 5
Place **2 salmon fillets** on or beside the vegetables. Brush with the glaze and top with slices from **1/2 lemon**.

#### Step 6
Roast for 10–14 mins, until the salmon flakes easily but is still juicy.

#### Step 7
Finish with extra **lemon juice** and fresh **dill or parsley**.

## Optional Lemon Butter Sauce

### Ingredients for Optional Lemon Butter Sauce

Ingredient | Quantity | How
---|---|---
Butter | 2 tbsp |
Garlic | 1 small clove | minced
Lemon | 1/2 | juiced
Dijon mustard | 1 tsp |
Water or cream | 1–2 tbsp |
Dill or parsley | to taste | chopped
Salt | to taste |
Pepper | to taste |

### Method for Optional Lemon Butter Sauce

#### Step 1
Melt **2 tbsp butter** gently in a small pan.

#### Step 2
Add **1 small garlic clove** and cook for 30 seconds.

#### Step 3
Stir in juice from **1/2 lemon**, **1 tsp Dijon mustard**, and **1–2 tbsp water or cream**. Simmer for 1 min.

#### Step 4
Add **dill or parsley**, **salt**, and **pepper** to taste.

## Notes:
Serve with rice, potatoes, couscous, bread, or as-is. For a lighter sauce, mix a few spoonfuls of **yoghurt or sour cream** with **lemon juice**, **salt**, **pepper**, and **dill**.
